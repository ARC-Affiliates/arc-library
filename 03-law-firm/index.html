Our Story

26 years.
200+ trials.

James Harmon founded this firm in 1998 after leaving the State Attorney's Office, where he prosecuted serious felonies. He knew from the other side of the aisle what it took to win at trial — and he built a defense and litigation firm around that knowledge.

Every attorney hired at Harmon & Associates is a trial attorney first. We do not hire settlement specialists. We do not hire attorneys who avoid the courtroom. The culture of this firm is built around one question: are you ready to try this case?

That posture has earned us the respect of judges, opposing counsel, and most importantly, our clients — whose freedom and futures have been entrusted to us over two and a half decades.

James R. Harmon
Founding Partner — 26 years at the bar
