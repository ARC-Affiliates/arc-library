Our Foundation

Thirty Years
of Singular
Purpose

"The finest properties in the world deserve representation that matches their rarity. That belief founded MAISON. It still drives everything we do."

Since 1994, MAISON has operated at the intersection of art, architecture, and ambition. We are not a brokerage — we are a private advisory firm for those who refuse to settle.

Philosophy

Built on Discretion,
Driven by Results

Every client relationship at MAISON begins with a single question: what does success look like to you? Not to the market. Not to us. To you. That question guides every recommendation, every negotiation, every decision we make on your behalf.

We have never chased volume. We have never compromised a client relationship for a commission. This is unusual in our industry. We consider it non-negotiable.

Our advisor roster is deliberately small. Each member of the MAISON team carries an average of 22 years of experience in ultra-high-net-worth real estate. They are former architects, art dealers, and estate attorneys — people who understand that buying a home at this level is never just a financial transaction.
